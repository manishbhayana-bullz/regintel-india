RegIntel India
Version 2.0 · Built 2026 · Live AI Engine
RegIntel India is an AI-powered regulatory compliance intelligence tool for the Indian BFSI sector. It reads RBI circulars, SEBI regulations, IRDAI guidelines, and DPDP Act provisions — and extracts what your team actually needs to do, in plain English, with deadlines, penalties, and responsible teams clearly mapped.
