RegIntel India
Version 1.0 · Built April 2026
RegIntel India is an AI-powered regulatory compliance intelligence tool built specifically for the Indian banking and financial services industry. It reads RBI circulars, SEBI regulations, and IRDAI guidelines and extracts what your team actually needs to do — in plain English, with deadlines and penalty amounts clearly highlighted.
